E-practical: Principle of Moments

This project reimagines the O level practical that uses
principle of moments to find out the weight of a metre rule.
The experiment is made smarter and automatic, as well as
easy to assemble for use at home as a kit.

Benefits our Kit Provides

What was the motivation behind the experiment revamp? Here are a few advantages the e-practical has over the traditional one.

Simple to Download

The students can download the lab code from the school website on their computer, and run it using the Arduino IDE.

Easy to Assemble

The kit comes with all the parts required for the experiment, and can be set up in just a few steps. No need for a lab to do science!

Automatic

The program keeps track of the experiment progress and gives instructions to the students.

Reusable

After the experiment is done, students can dismantle the kit for the next batch of students.
