The Apparatus

The majority of the components were made in 5mm acrylic because it was easy to work with and quick for prototyping. For components with more complex geometry, we used 3D printing.

Here is a list of the components at your disposal to carry out your experiment.

The beam The main component of the experiment, which weight will be determined at the end of the experiment. 1
The box Houses most of the electronics and used to measure the force acting on it using a load cell. You can adjust the height of the sensor to straigthen the beam using the handle on the side. You can connect your computer to the Arduino inside the box to carry out your experiment. 1
